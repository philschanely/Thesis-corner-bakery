Menu

Dozens of pastries are baked daily in house. Our selection ranges from lemon bars to muffins to loaves of bread.

A few of our items are seasonal, such as out pumpkin bread and out Christmas scones! Make sure you stop in to see what going on. Check out our menu to find out what else we have to offer.

Orders

Despite having fulfilling and wholesome pastries to buy in our bakery, we also offer you the opportunity for us to make and decorate cakes or cookies for you!

We have Lisa on hand, who has been creating cookie and cake masterpieces for over 10 years now. Stop in or give us a ring and talk with her about your vision and dreams and watch as the magic happens.

About Us

Corner Street Bakery is a local bakery that serves the community through delectable pastries and wonderful services.
